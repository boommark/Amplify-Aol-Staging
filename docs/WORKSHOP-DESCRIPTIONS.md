## Beginner Courses

### Happiness Program (Art of Living Part 1)
**Core technique:** Sudarshan Kriya (SKY) — a powerful rhythmic breathing technique.
**Duration:** 3 days, 2.5 hours/day. Daily practice after: ~20 minutes.
**Who it's for:** Complete beginners. No prerequisites.
**What participants learn:** SKY Breath Meditation, supporting pranayamas, interactive wisdom on the nature of mind and stress, guided processes for letting go of mental and emotional baggage.
**Key research:** 100+ independent studies. Yale study: SKY outperformed MBSR. 67-73% depression relief. 60% cortisol reduction in 3 months. Veterans: normalized anxiety in 1 week.
**Positioning:** The flagship entry point. Active breathwork (not passive meditation). Engages the nervous system directly. Gateway to all advanced courses. Lifetime access to weekly group practice worldwide.

### Sahaj Samadhi Meditation
**Core technique:** Effortless mantra-based meditation with a personalized mantra.
**Duration:** 3 days, 2 hours/day. Daily practice after: ~20 minutes.
**Who it's for:** Beginners and experienced meditators. No prerequisites (does NOT require Part 1).
**What participants learn:** A personal mantra received from a certified teacher. Effortless meditation without concentration, focus, or visualization. Accesses a "fourth state of consciousness."
**Key research:** RCT: 40% depression remission vs 16.3% control. Improved HRV. Best poster at 17th World Psychiatry Conference.
**Positioning:** Complements SKY (mantra-based vs breathwork). Fully independent after learning — no apps or guided audio needed. The meditation for people who've struggled with other forms.

### Art of Living Premium
**Core technique:** Both SKY and Sahaj Samadhi in a single bundled course.
**Duration:** 3 days, 3.5 hours/day. Daily practice after: ~40 minutes (both).
**Who it's for:** New participants who want both techniques. No prerequisites.
**Positioning:** Best value ($495 vs $590 separately). Saves time (3 days vs 6). Walk away with both an active breathwork practice AND a passive meditation practice. Compounding effect.

### Sri Sri Yoga Foundation Program
**Core technique:** Sri Sri Yoga — holistic yoga integrating traditional asanas, pranayama, meditation, and yogic wisdom.
**Duration:** 4-6 days, 2 hours/day. No prerequisites.
**What participants learn:** Traditional yoga postures for all levels, simple pranayamas, guided meditation, yogic knowledge, a personalized take-home yoga sequence.
**Positioning:** The only AOL beginner course focused on yoga asanas. Goes beyond physical postures to include breath, mind, and spirit. NOT the same as a gym yoga class. Does NOT teach SKY or Sahaj Samadhi. Accessible, non-judgmental, meets you where you are.

### Sleep and Anxiety Protocol
**Core technique:** Breathwork + NSDR/Yoga Nidra + sleep hygiene protocols.
**Duration:** 3 days, 2.5 hours/day (online only). Daily practice after: ~15 minutes.
**Who it's for:** People struggling with insomnia, poor sleep, or anxiety. No prerequisites.
**What participants learn:** Pranayamas for vagus nerve activation, NSDR/Yoga Nidra, circadian rhythm reset, practical sleep hygiene adjustments.
**Positioning:** Newest AOL course (launched 2024-2025). Targets root causes (nervous system dysregulation), not symptoms. Lowest price point (~$99). Lightest daily commitment (15 min). Drug-free, science-informed approach.

## Advanced Courses

### Art of Living Part 2 (Advanced Meditation / Art of Silence)
**Prerequisite:** Part 1 only.
**Duration:** 4-10 days. Silent retreat format.
**What it teaches:** "Hollow and Empty" guided meditations by Gurudev. Extended Sudarshan Kriya. Daily yoga and pranayama. Most of the course is in silence.
**Positioning:** The most common next step after Part 1. Profoundly deeper rest than daily practice. Silent retreat removes everyday distractions.

### Blessings Course
**Prerequisites:** Part 1 + 2x Part 2 + Sahaj Samadhi.
**Duration:** 3 days (online).
**What it teaches:** Meditation processes to reach elevated states, then channeling that energy into blessings and healing for others.
**Positioning:** Not about personal stress relief — about becoming an instrument of healing. One of the most advanced prerequisites in the AOL system.

### Chakra Kriya
**Prerequisites:** Part 1 + Part 2.
**Duration:** 1-hour online session.
**What it teaches:** Mudra techniques layered onto existing SKY practice to balance chakras.
**Positioning:** Shortest advanced course. An amplifier of what you already do, not a new technique.

### DSN (Divya Samaj Ka Nirman)
**Prerequisites:** Part 1 (Part 2 preferred).
**Duration:** 4 days, in-person.
**What it teaches:** Yoga, group processes, spiritual knowledge to break through personal inhibitions and fears. Includes Padmasadhana and sessions with Gurudev.
**Positioning:** Action-oriented personal empowerment, not meditation. Pushes comfort zones. Service orientation.

### Sanyam
**Prerequisites:** 4x Part 2 + Sahaj Samadhi + Patanjali Yoga Sutra study + physical fitness for 5 daily Padmasadhana rounds.
**Duration:** 5 days, retreat format.
**What it teaches:** Simultaneous practice of Dharana (concentration), Dhyana (meditation), and Samadhi (union) — the final three limbs of Patanjali's Ashtanga Yoga.
**Positioning:** The most advanced standard course in the AOL curriculum. Years of prior practice required.

### Shakti Kriya
**Prerequisites:** Part 1 + Part 2 + health screening.
**Duration:** 2 days, 2 hours/day (in-person).
**What it teaches:** Shakti Chakras, five elements, innate body rhythms, advanced pranayamas. Physically demanding.
**Positioning:** Short, concentrated energy upgrade. Health screening required due to physical intensity.

### Sri Sri Yoga Deep Dive (Level 2)
**Prerequisites:** Part 1.
**Duration:** 3-5 days, residential retreat.
**What it teaches:** Shankh Prakshalan (digestive cleanse), Jal Neti (nasal wash), advanced asanas, advanced pranayamas. Yogic detox.
**Positioning:** Physically intensive. Traditional Shatkarma purification. Reverses accumulated lifestyle damage. Prepares the body for deeper meditation.
