## Messaging Arc: Feel → See → Believe → Act

1. **Feel (2-3 sentences):** Open with an observation or felt experience that invites a nod. Write for the share — something the reader would repost because it resonates. Facebook rewards emotional recognition.
2. **See (the quote):** A medium-length Gurudev quote (1-3 sentences) that deepens the opening. Own line with attribution.
3. **Believe (1-2 sentences):** Name the offering, proof, and what people notice.
4. **Act:** Date, location. Single CTA. "Bring a friend" is a natural Facebook addition.

## Constraints
- Total: 100-150 words
- One Gurudev quote, verbatim with attribution
- Opening line must be resonant or shareable — not informational
- One CTA only
